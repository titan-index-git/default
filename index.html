🧠 What this means for you (Feb → Aug 2025)

✅ Top 3 Highlights

  • Wages are edging up: Avg Household Income climbs from ~$73.2k → ~$75.5k.
  • Jobs hold steady: Unemployment ~3.7–4.1%, ending at 3.7%.
  • Markets resilient: S&P 500 & NASDAQ trend higher overall, supporting 401(k)s.

⚠️ Top 3 Lowlights

  • Housing pain: 30-yr mortgage ~6.6%→6.9% and price-to-income stays >6×.
  • Essentials cost more: Gas and eggs rise; everyday prices feel sticky.
  • Mood is cautious: Consumer Confidence eases; presidential approval dips.

🧾 Who’s Better vs. Worse?

  • 💎 Better off: Upper class — shielded by wealth, investments, and property ownership.
  • 🏠 Squeezed: Middle class — wages rising, but housing and essentials outpace gains.
  • 💸 Worse off: Lower class — hardest hit, as inflation and rents eat away modest income gains.
